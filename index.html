AI工具应用的思考 Reflections on AI Tools Application

2026.02.05

在安克创新的这段时间，我深刻体会到AI不仅是工具，更是一种思维方式。从最初的尝试到现在熟练运用Nano Banana Pro创作内容，我发现关键在于如何提出正确的问题，如何将创意与技术结合... During my time at Anker, I realized AI is not just a tool, but a mindset. From initial attempts to proficiently using Nano Banana Pro, the key lies in asking the right questions and combining creativity with technology...

AI工具的价值不在于替代人的创造力，而在于放大创造力。我学会了用AI来快速迭代想法、优化文案、生成视觉方案，但最终的判断和调整仍然需要人的审美和洞察。 AI tools amplify rather than replace creativity. I learned to use AI for rapid ideation and content optimization, but final judgment requires human insight.

比如在制作"PP淘金计划"的推广内容时，我用AI生成了多个版本的文案，然后基于对团队成员的了解，选择最符合他们喜好的表达方式。这个过程让我意识到，AI是加速器，但人的同理心和理解力才是核心。 When creating "PP Gold Mining" campaign content, I generated multiple versions with AI, then selected based on team preferences. This taught me that AI is an accelerator, but empathy and understanding are core.

组织文化建设心得 Organizational Culture Building Insights

2026.01.28

策划了10多场部门文化活动后，我发现真正有效的文化建设，不是自上而下的灌输，而是创造让员工主动参与、真心认同的场景和机制... After organizing 10+ events, I learned that effective culture building isn't top-down indoctrination, but creating scenarios where employees actively participate and genuinely identify...

"与我对话""定格温暖"这些栏目的诞生，源于我对员工真实需求的观察。大家需要的不是高高在上的宣讲，而是平等的对话、温暖的记录、有趣的互动。 Columns like "Talk to Me" and "Warm Moments" emerged from observing real employee needs. People want equal dialogue, warm records, and engaging interactions.

当员工参与率达到90%、满意度提升到70%时，我意识到文化建设的本质是让每个人感受到被看见、被重视。这也是我做内容的初心——用心感受用户，用内容连接人心。 When participation reached 90% and satisfaction hit 70%, I realized culture building is about making everyone feel seen and valued. This is my core principle - connecting hearts through content.

从62.5万阅读量学到的内容密码 Content Secrets from 625K Views

2026.01.15

我的一篇笔记达到了62.5万阅读量、1.5万点赞。好内容不是华丽的辞藻，不是刻意的煽情，而是真诚、共鸣和价值... One post reached 625K views and 15K likes. Great content isn't about fancy words or deliberate sentimentality, but authenticity, resonance, and value...

那篇内容之所以成功，是因为我写的是自己的真实经历和感受，而这种真实恰好触动了很多人的共同经历。我学到了：好内容不是追热点，而是找到自己与用户的情感连接点。 The content succeeded because I shared authentic experiences that resonated with many. Great content isn't chasing trends, but finding emotional connections with users.

完读率、互动率这些数据背后，是一个个真实的人。做内容的本质，是理解人性、洞察需求、提供价值。这也是我一直坚持的创作理念。 Behind completion and engagement metrics are real people. Content creation is fundamentally about understanding humanity, identifying needs, and providing value.
