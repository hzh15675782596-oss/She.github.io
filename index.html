AI工具应用的深度思考 Deep Thoughts on AI Tools

2026.02

在安克创新的实习中，我深刻认识到AI不仅是效率工具，更是一种重塑创作范式的力量...

At Anker, I deeply understood that AI is not just an efficiency tool, but a force reshaping creative paradigms...

从第一次使用Nano Banana Pro生成内容开始，我经历了从惊艳到质疑、从依赖到理性使用的完整心路历程。最初，我惊叹于AI能在几秒钟内产出完整的文案和视觉方案，这种"魔法般"的效率让我一度以为内容创作的门槛会被彻底打破。但很快我发现，没有人类审美和策略思维的AI输出，往往只是华丽的空壳。 From first using Nano Banana Pro, I experienced the complete journey from amazement to questioning, from dependence to rational use. Initially, I was amazed by AI's ability to produce complete content in seconds, but I soon realized that AI output without human aesthetics and strategic thinking is often just an ornate shell.

真正的转折点是"PP淘金计划"项目。我用AI生成了20多个版本的推广文案，但最终采用的版本，是我基于对团队文化的理解、对目标受众心理的洞察，从AI的输出中提取灵感、重新组织语言后的成果。那一刻我明白了：AI是放大器，它放大的是使用者的洞察力、审美力和判断力。你输入什么样的prompt，背后藏着什么样的思考深度，最终就会得到什么样的结果。 The real turning point was the "PP Gold Mining" project. I generated 20+ versions with AI, but the final version was my work based on understanding team culture and audience psychology, extracting inspiration from AI outputs and reorganizing the language. That's when I understood: AI is an amplifier that magnifies the user's insight, aesthetics, and judgment.

现在，我把AI视为一个"思维伙伴"而非"替代品"。它帮我快速验证想法、打破思维定势、探索多种可能性，但最终的创意方向、情感温度、文化适配，都需要我作为人类创作者去把控。这种"人机协作"的模式，让我的创作效率提升了300%，同时保持了内容的温度和深度。 Now, I see AI as a "thinking partner" rather than a "replacement." It helps me quickly validate ideas, break thinking patterns, and explore possibilities, but the final creative direction, emotional warmth, and cultural fit require me as a human creator to control. This "human-AI collaboration" model increased my creation efficiency by 300% while maintaining content warmth and depth.

组织文化建设的实践心得 Organizational Culture Building Insights

2026.01

策划10+场文化活动，推动参与率从30%到90%的过程中，我发现文化建设的本质是"让人被看见"...

Through organizing 10+ cultural events and driving participation from 30% to 90%, I discovered that culture building is essentially about "making people feel seen"...

刚接手部门文化建设时，我面对的是一个尴尬的局面：员工满意度只有45%，大家对各种活动参与度极低，普遍反映"感觉是在完成任务，没有真正的投入感"。我开始反思：为什么精心策划的活动反而让人疲惫？症结在哪里？ When I first took over departmental culture building, I faced an awkward situation: employee satisfaction was only 45%, participation in various activities was extremely low, and people generally felt they were "just completing tasks without real engagement." I began to reflect: why do carefully planned activities make people tired? Where is the crux?

我花了整整一周时间，逐一访谈了部门20多位员工，倾听他们的真实想法。我发现问题的核心不是活动形式，而是"自上而下的单向传播"模式。员工需要的不是被告知"公司文化是什么"，而是有机会表达"我是谁、我在意什么"。基于这个洞察，我设计了"与我对话"栏目——让每个员工成为主角，讲述自己的故事、分享自己的观点。 I spent a whole week interviewing more than 20 department employees one by one, listening to their real thoughts. I found that the core problem was not the activity format, but the "top-down one-way communication" model. Employees don't need to be told "what company culture is," but need opportunities to express "who I am and what I care about." Based on this insight, I designed the "Talk to Me" column - making each employee the protagonist, telling their own stories and sharing their own views.

结果令人惊喜：第一期栏目推出后，参与率就达到了60%，很多平时不爱发言的同事主动投稿。"定格温暖"照片征集活动更是引发了全员的情感共鸣——当大家看到自己日常工作中的温暖瞬间被记录、被看见、被珍视时，那种归属感是自然而然生发的。最终，部门满意度从45%提升到70%，参与率稳定在90%以上。 The results were surprising: after the first issue was launched, the participation rate reached 60%, and many colleagues who usually don't speak up actively submitted contributions. The "Warm Moments" photo collection activity even triggered emotional resonance among all staff - when people saw their warm moments in daily work being recorded, seen, and cherished, that sense of belonging arose naturally. Ultimately, department satisfaction increased from 45% to 70%, with participation stabilizing above 90%.

这段经历让我深刻理解了一个道理：真正的文化建设不是"灌输价值观"，而是"创造场域"——一个让每个人都能表达自我、感受温暖、建立连接的场域。当人们感到被看见、被重视、被理解时，文化认同就会自然发生。这也成为我后来做所有内容工作的底层逻辑：始终以"人"为中心，始终关注"人的感受"。 This experience taught me a profound truth: real culture building is not about "instilling values," but about "creating a field" - a field where everyone can express themselves, feel warmth, and build connections. When people feel seen, valued, and understood, cultural identity occurs naturally. This also became the underlying logic of all my content work: always "people-centered," always focused on "people's feelings."

内容创作的本质理解 Understanding Content Creation Essence

2025.07

从62.5万阅读量、1.5万点赞的爆款笔记中，我提炼出了内容创作的三个关键词：真诚、共鸣、价值...

From a viral post with 625K views and 15K likes, I distilled three keywords for content creation: authenticity, resonance, value...

那篇获得62.5万阅读量的笔记，其实并非我精心策划的"爆款设计"，而是一次真实经历的记录。我分享了自己从传统媒体转型到AI内容创作的心路历程、遇到的困惑和突破的瞬间。没有刻意的标题党，没有煽情的金句，只是坦诚地讲述"我是谁、我经历了什么、我学到了什么"。 The post that got 625K views was not a carefully planned "viral design," but a record of real experience. I shared my journey from traditional media to AI content creation, the confusion I encountered and breakthrough moments. No deliberate clickbait, no sentimental quotes, just honestly telling "who I am, what I experienced, what I learned."

但正是这种"不刻意"，反而引发了巨大的共鸣。评论区里，有刚毕业的应届生说"看到了自己的迷茫"，有转型期的职场人说"找到了方向"，还有创业者说"感受到了同频的能量"。我意识到，真正打动人的内容，不是高高在上的"指导"，而是平等的"陪伴"——我愿意暴露自己的脆弱、分享自己的真实，读者也愿意敞开心扉与你产生连接。 But it was precisely this "non-deliberateness" that triggered huge resonance. In the comments, fresh graduates said "I saw my own confusion," career transitioners said "I found direction," and entrepreneurs said "I felt resonating energy." I realized that truly moving content is not condescending "guidance," but equal "companionship" - I am willing to expose my vulnerability and share my truth, and readers are willing to open up and connect with you.

从这次经历中，我提炼出了内容创作的"三真"法则：真实的自我、真诚的表达、真正的价值。真实的自我，意味着不伪装、不扮演，敢于展示不完美；真诚的表达，意味着用人话说人话，而不是堆砌术语和概念；真正的价值，意味着给读者带来启发、陪伴或实用的帮助，而不仅仅是消费他们的注意力。 From this experience, I distilled the "Three Truths" principle of content creation: authentic self, sincere expression, real value. Authentic self means not pretending or performing, daring to show imperfection; sincere expression means speaking in human language, not piling up jargon and concepts; real value means bringing readers inspiration, companionship or practical help, not just consuming their attention.

现在，无论是写一篇小红书笔记、策划一场部门活动、还是制作一份AI内容，我都会先问自己三个问题：这是我真实的想法吗？这能与读者/用户产生情感共鸣吗？这能给他们带来真正的价值吗？只有当三个答案都是"是"时，我才会动手创作。这个标准虽然严格，但让我的内容完读率稳定在85%以上，粉丝粘性持续提升。因为人们能感知到真诚，也愿意为真诚买单。 Now, whether writing a Xiaohongshu note, planning a department event, or creating AI content, I first ask myself three questions: Is this my real thought? Can this emotionally resonate with readers/users? Can this bring them real value? Only when all three answers are "yes" will I start creating. This standard, though strict, has kept my content completion rate stable above 85%, with continuously increasing fan loyalty. Because people can perceive sincerity and are willing to pay for it.
